#Requirement #1 Resources:

  1. For one day, keep a log in which you describe your communications activities. Worksheet
  2. For three days, keep a journal of your listening experiences. Worksheet
  3. In a small group setting, meet with other Scouts or friends. Have them share personal experiences about significant events in their lives that affected them in some way.
  4. List as many ways as you can think of to communicate with others. Worksheet
